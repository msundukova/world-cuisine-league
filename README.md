# Russian (Soviet) recipe: a starter
* Fish under the fur coat (Shuba)*

## Ingredients

Vegetables:

* 2/3 beetroots (remolacha) - already cooked and under vaccum
* 2 carrots
* 2 potatoes
* 1/2 or 1 sweet onion/ chalotte


Favourite dressing: 
* sour cream
* OR mayonnaise (can be light)
* Or greek yogurt + soya


Fish:
* pickled herring / sardinas arenques (link)
* smoked/salted salmon (link)
* boquerones de anchoas?

For decoration (optional):
* hard-boiled egg
* a bit of parsley

Serving of this layered salad:
* flat serving dish
* (optional) serving ring
* OR transparent glass (like Nutella size)
* OR transparent jar (medium, up to 250 ml volume)


Vegetarian/ vegan substitions:
* pickled cucumber instead of fish
* vegan variant of dressing (vegannaise?)
