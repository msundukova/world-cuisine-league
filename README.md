# Russian (Soviet) recipe: a starter *Fish under a fur coat (aka Shuba)*
it is a layered salad made of vegetables and pickeled herring

## Ingredients

Vegetables:

* 2 or 3 beetroots ("remolacha" in Spanish) - e.g. cooked and vaccum packed (1 pack)
* 2 carrots
* 2 potatoes
* 1/2 or 1 sweet onion / shallot

Fish:
* (original) pickled herring 
* (improvised) smoked/salted salmon (100 - 150 g)
* (improvised #2) anchovies in the form of Boquerones en vinagre 


 Dressing: 
* (original) mayonnaise
* (improvised) sour cream + light mayonnaise
* (improvised #2) greek-type yogurt + a bit of soya sauce

For decoration (optional):
* 1 hard-boiled egg
* a bit of parsley
* a bit of spring onion

For serving of this layered salad:
* flat serving dish
* (optional) serving ring
* (improvised) transparent glass (like Nutella size)
* (improvised) transparent jar (medium, up to 250 ml volume)

Vegetarian/ vegan substitions:
* Use pickled cucumber instead of fish
* Use your favourite vegan variant of dressing (e.g. egg-free )
